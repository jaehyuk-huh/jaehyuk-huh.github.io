Short Bio

Jaehyuk Huh is a professor of School of Computing at KAIST, and co-leads CASYS (Computer Architecture and Systems) Lab. His research interests are in computer architecture, systems for AI, and hardware support for system security. He received his PhD in computer science from the University of Texas at Austin and worked for Advanced Micro Devices before joining KAIST in 2008.
